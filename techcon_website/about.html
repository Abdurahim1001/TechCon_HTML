History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Every year, it brings together innovators, developers, and industry leaders to share the latest advancements and inspire the next generation of tech professionals.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, promote collaboration, and empower the global tech community. We aim to bridge the gap between visionaries and developers, helping to bring ideas to life and create a better technological future for all.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, delivered a groundbreaking keynote in 2015, where he shared his vision of space exploration and sustainable energy.

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, spoke at TechCon 2018 about the future of AI and machine learning, and how it is shaping the world of technology.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Meta, was a keynote speaker in 2019. She discussed leadership in the tech industry and how to foster inclusivity and innovation.
